Our Story

The Li Institution was originally created back in May 2022 by Liangyue Zhao and Neng Li. Anthony Huang joined soon afterwards. When it was first created, the Li Institution had no specific goals or objectives, and it does not right now either. We're just a bunch of people like you and me, making open-sourced software and apps because we wanted to!

Our Mission
